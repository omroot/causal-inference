# Causal Inference Literature Review

A curated collection of books, tutorials, and resources for studying causal inference.

**Author:** Oualid Missaoui

## Schools of Thought

There are two main, prominent schools of thought for causal inference:

- **Graphical Models** - Specifically, Structural Causal Models (SCMs) with Directed Acyclic Graphs (DAGs) and the do-calculus, pioneered by Judea Pearl
- **Potential Outcomes Framework** - Also known as the Rubin Causal Model (RCM), developed by Donald Rubin
